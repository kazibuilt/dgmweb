ERPNext

What is ERPNext?

ERPNext is an open-source web-based integrated ERP system. It is built on the MariaDB database using Frappe, a Python-based server-side framework.

It is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and integrate various aspects of your business operations into a single, cloud-based platform.

Unlike traditional ERP systems that are based on legacy software and rely on in-house servers, ERPNext leverages the power of the internet and remote data centers to provide real-time access to critical business data from anywhere, at any time.
