CosmOS

The OS for the Intelligence Age.

sign up to build with us

CosmOS is the operating system built for AI. By understanding your preferences and needs, it provides personalized assistance, streamlines interactions, and handles complex tasks across all your devices.

What CosmOS Does

CosmOS intelligently orchestrates advanced agents and Large Language Models (LLM) to create a seamless, context-aware experience.

Intelligent Agents For Every Tasks

Intelligent agents are at the core of CosmOS. Agents are specialized AI programs designed to handle specific tasks and work together seamlessly. They operate in the background without the need for direct management. These agents are the driving force behind CosmOS’ ability to understand context, anticipate needs, and execute complex operations.

Each agent is an expert in its domain — weather, news, music, vision, communication, task planning, etc. They work together, sharing information and coordinating efforts to tackle multi-step challenges.

Personalized & Contextual

CosmOS evolves and becomes personalized over time by learning from your interactions and history and staying contextually relevant with up to date data from the web and information you provide.

The Ai Bus: An Intelligent Orchestrator

At the heart of CosmOS lies the Ai Bus, a powerful AI orchestration system. This intelligent conductor seamlessly coordinates various AI models, data sets, services, and device capabilities to deliver a fluid, intuitive experience. From your device to LLMs to any API in between, CosmOS handles complex requests with ease.

Multistep Reasoning & Parallel Processing

CosmOS breaks down requests into multiple steps and directs them to specialized agents ensuring they have the necessary information to process tasks accurately. The Ai Bus coordinates how results and information are shared across agents while simultaneously managing multiple requests in parallel.

CosmOS understands the context and intent behind your requests. When agents come back with results, CosmOS makes sure they’re appropriate, accurate and keeps processing if the task isn’t done yet.

Intelligent Error Handling

If an agent encounters an issue, our sophisticated orchestration finds alternative sources — minimizing errors and maximizing results.

Scalable & Adaptable

CosmOS is designed to support multiple device form factors and adapts to the latest advancements in AI technologies — making it easy for new agents and capabilities to come on board.

Multimodal Control

CosmOS will understand your intent and orchestrate the right inputs that are available on your device. Native multimodal support on CosmOS means that you can use multiple input methods such as, voice, touch, text, gesture or vision — and combine or switch between them midstream.

Designed for Everything

CosmOS is designed to work across devices, adapting seamlessly to a wide range of form factors and interaction modalities. This flexibility allows CosmOS to run on different device types, from large professional workstations to personal wearable technology.

CosmOS takes mobile computing to the next level with deeply context-aware devices that understand and interact with the world around them.

The user interface adapts to the user’s cognitive load and environmental factors, ensuring an optimal experience in any situation. These mobile devices also feature enhanced sensory capabilities for natural and immersive interactions with the physical world, including designed for everything object recognition and interaction capabilities.

Build for Everyone

The CosmOS ecosystem is designed to be open, extensible, and collaborative, fostering innovation and allowing for deep customization to meet diverse needs.

At its core is the CosmOS Software Development Kit (SDK), a comprehensive set of tools, libraries, and APIs that enable developers to create agents and services that seamlessly integrate with the CosmOS operating system and live alongside the suite of AI services.

The SDK provides robust APIs for accessing CosmOS core functionalities, including AI capabilities and context-aware features.

Developers can utilize tools for creating experiences that work across various form factors, ensuring a consistent user experience regardless of the device.

Libraries for integrating with CosmOS’ personal memory and identity management systems allow applications to leverage the user's personal data securely and efficiently.

Privacy First, Trust Always

In the CosmOS ecosystem, data privacy and ownership are not just features, but fundamental principles that underpin the entire system.

CosmOS strengthens the relationship between users, their data, and technology providers, putting control firmly in the hands of individuals and organizations.

For businesses and organizations, CosmOS provides robust tools for managing sensitive corporate data while maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements.

IT administrators have access to centralized data governance controls, allowing them to set and enforce data policies across the organization.

Customizable data classification and access policies help ensure that sensitive information is properly protected and only accessible to authorized personnel.

For the most sensitive information, secure authorization can provide an additional layer of protection, ensuring that highly confidential data is processed in isolated, secure environments.
