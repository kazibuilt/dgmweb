A Concept built one of its kind

Choreography Online is one of its kind platform that we have worked upon. You won’t find many (if any) platforms similar to it which allow dancers to license their choreography. Our developers took up this concept as a challenge and built a well-funneled platform and marketplace of choreography.

User optimized design

The complete project is designed considering the newness of the concept to prospective dancers and license buyers. It is made cross-device accessible as well as frictionless for the new users.
