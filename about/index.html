A story of construction, technology, and bike pedals.

When you find an intersection between your interests.

Interesting things happen when your passions intersect. As construction buffs who grew into engineers, we toyed with mixing building materials and technology. We had the basic ingredients for something new, but then came the spark. What if framing clips could snap into place as easily as cycling shoes click into a bike pedal?

We were off to the races. Along the way, we found that software would play an important role. Whereas most engineers use software to build virtual worlds, we harness it to build the real world. To solve real problems in an industry which benefits all of us. Less waste. Higher productivity. More affordability.

Hyperframe is here. And it’s extremely practical.

Our team works as hard as our products do.
