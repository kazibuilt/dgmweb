Managing a plan

If you need to pause your plan, you may do so for one billing cycle and continue to have access until your current billing cycle ends. Once paused, your device will be in a blocked state but your data will be accessible within your account. If you have a change of heart, simply unpause.

Once you setup your Humane Plan it can be managed directly in your Account under the Plan & Billing section. Review data usage, billing statements, update your payment method and sign up for Premium add-ons at your convenience.

Canceling a plan

The Humane Plan may be canceled at any time. If the cancelation is submitted prior to your next billing date, you will have access through the end of the current billing cycle. After the billing cycle ends, your plan will be canceled. While you will still have access to the data within your account, the device will be inoperable. If you do not reactivate your Humane Plan within 60 days, your phone number will be rescinded.
