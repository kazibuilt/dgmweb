    01 Formulation discovery overview

    Get Started

    Our world-class scientists harness ZYA™ to design and optimize formulations utilizing our internal database of hundreds of industry-tested ingredients and patented erthos® Ingredients developed in-house. ZYA™ predicts the capabilities and behavior of novel ingredients while generating formulations for our client's desired targets across a wide range of end applications. ZYA™ insights, ingredients, and formulations are shared transparently with our clients.

    02 Material development overview

    Get started

    erthos® Lab conducts rapid prototyping of novel formulations discovered through ZYA™. Our in-house experts conduct real-world testing and manufacturing compatibility trials at scale. All insights, formulations, and testing results are translated into final material samples ready for commercialization.
