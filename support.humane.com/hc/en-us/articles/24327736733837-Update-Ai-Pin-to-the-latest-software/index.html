Update Ai Pin to the latest software

How it works

Your Ai Pin automatically updates with the latest software as long as the following conditions are met:

  • Ai Pin + Booster placed on the Charge Pad.
  • If Ai Pin has been powered off, you've unlocked it at least once since powering on.
  • Ai Pin and Battery Booster power levels are 80% or higher. (Tap the Charge Pad while Pin + Booster is on it. You will see a green light if power is 80% or higher.)

We recommend charging your Pin + Booster on the Charge Pad overnight to automatically receive software updates and avoid any interruptions during the process. 

Check for software updates

If there is an available over-the-air (OTA) software update, a "software update" message will appear at the top of the settings menu. If you don’t see "software update" in the settings menu, your Pin has the most recent software. To check for this message:

  1. Tap the touchpad with one finger to indicate you’d like to activate Laser Ink. 
  2. Open your hand. You should see the Trust Light flashing white to indicate your Ai Pin is scanning for your hand.
  3. Once detected, the Laser Ink Display will project on your hand.
  4. Push out your hand a few more inches until you see the menu.
  5. Pick settings .
  6. If you see 'Software update,' there is a new version waiting to be installed. Place your Ai Pin and Battery Booster on the Charge Pad to update overnight.

Check software version

Using voice

  1. Press and hold one finger on the touchpad. You will see the Trust Light flashing orange and hear a chime indicating Ai Mic is listening. 
  2. Say “What's my software version," or something similar. 
  3. Release finger from the touchpad. The Trust Light will stop flashing and you’ll hear a chime indicating Ai Mic is no longer listening.
  4. Ai Pin will answer with the current version. 

Using Laser Ink

  1. Tap the touchpad with one finger to indicate you’d like to activate Laser Ink.
  2. Open your hand. You should see the Trust Light flashing white to indicate your Ai Pin is scanning for your hand.
  3. Once detected, the Laser Ink Display will project on your hand. 
  4. Push out your hand a few more inches until you see the menu.
  5. Pick settings .  
  6. Scroll and pick "About."
  7. See "Version."
