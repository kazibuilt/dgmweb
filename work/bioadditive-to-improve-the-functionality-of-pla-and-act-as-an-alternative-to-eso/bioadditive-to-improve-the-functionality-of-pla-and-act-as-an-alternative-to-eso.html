    Introducing DGM® Plasticizer, an innovative additive designed to replace ESO (Epoxidized Soybean Oil) in flexible materials, offering a sustainable alternative for clients seeking environmentally friendly solutions. Our proprietary formula enhances the flexibility, durability, and performance of materials while reducing reliance on non-renewable resources.

    Project highlights

    • Composition

      100% biobased

    • Compost impact

      100% compostable

    • Material make-up

      0% harsh chemicals, mineral acids, catalysts.

    • Key takeaway

      Significant enhancement of elongation and reduction of brittleness.

    ProcessProcess 

    Our journey began with in-depth discussions with our confidential client to understand their specific needs and sustainability goals. Leveraging our expertise in sustainable additives, we developed DGM® Plasticizer, a bio-based alternative to ESO, tailored to meet the client's requirements. Through rigorous testing and optimization, we ensured that the additive enhanced the flexibility and durability of the client's materials while reducing their environmental footprint. We then supported the client in integrating DGM® Plasticizer into their manufacturing process seamlessly, providing ongoing technical assistance to ensure a smooth transition.
