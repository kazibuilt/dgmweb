    Our company

    At DGM®, we harness the power of AI, biomaterials, and global impact to reimagine the building blocks of plastics. Through DGM® Studio, our AI-powered design studio, we develop bespoke biomaterials for the world's largest CPGs and material companies tailored to their sustainable and functional goals with our proprietary DGM® Technologies, including DGM® Ingredients and our in-house machine learning AI platform, ZYA™.
